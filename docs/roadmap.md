# Roadmap

1. Building management: manage buildings that host elevator screens.
2. Screen management: register and manage elevator screens and their status.
3. Advertiser management: manage advertiser accounts and basic profile data.
4. Creative management: upload, review, and approve ad creatives.
5. Campaign management: define campaigns, budgets, targeting, and schedules.
6. Campaign creative assignment: associate approved creatives with campaigns.
7. Campaign delivery constraints: define when, where, and how campaign creatives may run.
8. Daily playlist generation: build each screen's scheduled sequence for the day.
9. Playlist download endpoint: allow the screen or player to fetch its current daily schedule.
10. Playback / proof-of-play reporting: record what the screen actually played after execution.
11. Basic delivery reports: expose campaign, playlist, and playback reporting.
12. SSP/DSP concepts: introduce supply-side and demand-side workflow concepts later.
13. OpenRTB later: add OpenRTB compatibility only after the scheduled playlist model is stable.
